ONCE A FILMMAKER...

DIEGO PEREZ V — ALWAYS A FILMMAKER

I love making films. The generic origin story, making films since I was a kid. Well... teen. I didn't have access to a camera as a kid. Anyways, what can I say? I love the process, shooting, framing, directing. It's kind of funny, because I haven't seen a lot of movies, which is a big awkward. The truth is I think I love making films more than I love watching them, although I will say that I also prefer watching them in theaters, on the BIG SCREEN, so maybe that's a reason why I haven't seen so many "must-sees". Last year I shot my first short film in A WHILE. After moving to a new city I'm just trying to get back in the game.
